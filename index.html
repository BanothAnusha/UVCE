UVCE Hihglights

First Engineering College of Karnataka

Established in 1917 under the name Government Engineering College, it is the fifth engineering college in the country.


Vision of Sir M Visvesvaraya

In order to develop a skilled force among the boys from Mysore, BharatRatna Sir M Visvesvaraya envisioned the college in 1917.

100 Years of Legacy

What started off with 20 students in civil and mechanical branches, now has over 3300 students in 7 different branches.


Campus Location

The main campus is situated at KR Circle, in neighbourhood of Vidhana Soudha, while the other is situated amidst Jnana Bharati Campus spread over 50 acres

World-Wide Recognition

Skill Development

The college focuses on overall development of students comprising of both academic and extra curricular activities.


Campus Recruitment

The college has an excellent Industry - Institution interaction with all the reputed Global Companies visiting the campus


Research

Most students are involved in Research and have published Technical papers and also been acknowledged in International Conferences and Journals in the last few years.


Great Environment

The students of this college are hardworking, cultured, highly disciplined and have preserved the cherished values of this institution over the years.


Hostel Facilties

The College provides hostel facilities for boys and girls separately both in the Jnana Bharathi and City Campus to reduce the travel time.
